Number Generator

Set Up Your Session

Assign a different variation or movement to each number. When a number appears, perform that variation while playing. Browse the Exercise Ideas below for inspiration.

How does this work? ▸
Quick Guide:
1. Choose how many variations your teacher assigned (e.g. 4 means numbers 1–4).
2. Set how fast numbers change — slower for beginners, faster for a challenge.
3. Set a session duration.
4. Each time a number appears, perform the matching variation while playing your piece.

Tip: Use "2 numbers at once" to combine two types of variations simultaneously for advanced practice.

Coordination Exercises

Coordination Chart

Play a memorized piece while reading and executing the movements shown in the chart. Each cell tells you what to do with a specific body part — the color tells you which part, the letter tells you the action. Select which body parts to include, then generate a chart. For an extra challenge, try doing the opposite of what the chart says, or swap the meaning of foot colors.
